Toni Mertanen

Work

As a devoted father of four delightful daughters, I'm a tech enthusiast with a diverse background in entrepreneurship and a passion for learning. My professional journey started over two decades ago in the retail industry, where I gained expertise in human resource and data-driven management, account and assortment management, stakeholder collaboration, and customer service. I'm also an avid learner who is currently exploring the vast world of artificial intelligence and coding to find more efficient ways of working.

My professional journey commenced when I was a seventeen-year-old college student and was discovered right outside the doorstep of my family's apartment. From the bottle and cart boy in the beginning to present work as a K-retailer entrepreneur I have done all the different jobs in a grocery store one possibly can.

Throughout my career I have had the privilege of working with numerous superiors and collaborators and with two different K-retailers. These opportunities have enabled me to develop the expertise to work effectively with various individuals and teams. As an entrepreneur for over a decade, I have gained invaluable experience in leading both employees and teams. Some of my key entrepreneurial qualities include the ability to withstand stress, make swift decisions and work with a solution-based approach.

When I started in the retail industry in February 1997, ordering and assortment management were mostly manual - orders were made by calling and the quantity was always decided by the purchaser. However, at that time, there were already portable devices available that transmitted orders electronically to suppliers.

The operations of the retail industry inevitably began to utilize more information technology, and soon we were able to enjoy the first time when orders were made by the machine. However this had nothing to do with artificial intelligence - the algorithms were simple at that time.

It has always been easy for me to adopt information technology as part of my daily work. I have been interested in what can be found under the hood of an application or a system - why something works the way it does. Nowadays, most of our tools are cloud based and are used on mobile devices or computers. As part of my studies, I did an internship in the Kesko artificial intelligence unit and after studying the Python programming language used by the unit day and night, I eventually managed to code somewhat independently. Today, a small part of the code in the assortment management application used by every K-food store was written by me.

Studies

I have always loved different kind of systems, computers and gadgets. The desire to find out what is the logic behind them and the fast growing digitalization in the field of grocery trade - especially inside K group - drove me to apply to LAB University of Applied Sciences. After passing the entrance exams I found myself studying to become an engineer of information and communication technology. All the studies were remote studies and I have studied little less than two years. It was of course sometimes a bit hard to combine work, studies and family life.

Recent global events such as the COVID-19 pandemic and the war in Ukraine have impacted business operations in a way that required me to be more involved in my company's work. The second year of studies was also more intense than the first and it began to be difficult to study efficiently while working - however, it is always important to remember what brings bread to the table. I had to put my studies on hold, but I have continued to study independently through online courses. I have taken all the possible free moments to study. I have acquired many courses from internet to advance the studies taken in school and many topics outside the school courses.

Currently I am studying the Elements of AI online course created by the University of Helsinki and MinnaLearn. I have already completed the first part of the course - Introduction to AI - and now I am working on the second part of the course, Building AI. I studied online courses for Python programming language and SQL databases once more to facilitate the completion of the Elements of AI course.

The release of ChatGPT for public use in late 2022 made the world realize just how advanced AI models have become. My interest in AI led me to learn more about the topic beyond just reading news headlines, and our first encounter extended into the late hours of the night. Even my studies and fascination with systems and computers did not prepare me for what I discovered. In that moment I realized that this was something that would change the world significantly. From that moment on I have been slowly studying and testing what ChatGPT is capable of. The next step through ChatGPT of course was to start learning about how GitHub Copilot can help me in my coding. It was clear that in order to use artificial intelligence you must have a foundational understanding of the subject matter to which you are applying AI. Otherwise you cannot critically examine the solution options provided by the AI.

My current goal is to combine the knowledge I have gained from both my studies and hobbies to achieve more efficient ways of working - for example I spend a lot of time at work going through large amounts of data to manage assortment. By coding a bit I could automate these everyday tasks and in the end even let artificial intelligence perform them.
